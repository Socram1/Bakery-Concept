Our Story

A little bakery with a big heart.

Little Pastane started on a Sunday morning with a croissant recipe and a dream. Every item we make is folded, filled, and finished by hand — no shortcuts, no shortcuts, no compromises.

We source local butter, seasonal fruit, and stone-milled flour because the best bakes start with the best ingredients.
